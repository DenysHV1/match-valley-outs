About This Game

Match Valley reimagines the match-3 genre by combining it with RPG-style hero development and whimsical storytelling. Each puzzle level isn’t just about clearing the board — it’s about assembling your team, upgrading their skills, and holding back waves of quirky invaders threatening your kingdom.

Players match symbols to activate abilities, unlock upgrades, and trigger powerful chain reactions. Your team grows stronger with each victory, allowing you to explore new valleys, uncover secrets, and face increasingly clever foes. With an offline mode, regular events, and new valleys added frequently, Match Valley is built to keep you engaged — whether for five minutes or five hours.

How to play

Match 3 or more identical tiles to clear them from the board and charge your heroes’ special abilities.

Deploy your heroes as their power fills. Each hero has a unique effect — from explosive attacks to healing buffs.

Use boosters strategically to clear obstacles like chains or blockers that hinder your progress.

Upgrade your team between levels with earned points or coins to tackle tougher enemies in the next valley.

Complete valley episodes to unlock new characters, scenes, and bonus rewards.

FAQ

  • Do I need an internet connection to play?

    No, Match Valley is fully playable offline. However, connecting online lets you compete in leaderboards and earn special rewards.

  • How do I unlock new heroes?

    Heroes can be unlocked by progressing through levels, opening chests, or earning rewards in limited-time events.

  • What do power-ups do?

    Power-ups like Rainbow Bombs or Magic Wands help you clear large sections of the board, break through blockers, or trigger extra turns.

  • What happens if I lose a level?

    You can retry the level at no cost. Using boosters or upgrading your heroes may help on the next attempt.

  • Are there ads or in-app purchases?

    The game is free-to-play with optional purchases. Ads may appear but can be removed with certain packages.
