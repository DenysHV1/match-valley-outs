“Fun with a twist”

I’ve played dozens of match-3 games, but Match Valley brings something fresh. Matching to power up your heroes adds a strategic edge. Level 34 was a challenge with its tricky blockers, but after upgrading my healer, I made it through.

This is my go-to game during breaks. It’s not too intense but always engaging. I love how each new valley introduces different enemies. Level 51 stumped me until I discovered how to chain combos.
